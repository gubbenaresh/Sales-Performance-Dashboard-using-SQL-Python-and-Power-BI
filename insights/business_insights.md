# Business Insights

## 1. Sales Performance

Total sales and total profit were calculated to understand overall business performance. Average order value was also calculated to measure how much customers spend per order.

## 2. Category Performance

Category-wise sales and profit analysis helped identify which product categories generated the highest revenue and profit.

## 3. Regional Performance

Region-wise analysis showed which regions contributed most to total sales and profit.

## 4. Monthly Trend

Monthly sales and profit trends were analyzed to understand business growth over time and seasonal performance.

## 5. Product Performance

Top-selling products were identified based on total sales. Loss-making products were also identified to understand products that reduce profitability.

## 6. Customer Analysis

Top customers were identified based on sales contribution. This can help businesses target high-value customers with loyalty programs.

## 7. Discount Analysis

Discount and profit were compared to understand whether higher discounts were reducing profitability.

## 8. Segment Analysis

Customer segment analysis helped identify which segment contributes the most to sales and profit.

## 9. Shipping Analysis

Shipping mode performance was analyzed to understand sales, profit, and average shipping time across different shipping methods.

## Final Conclusion

This project shows how SQL, Python, and Power BI can be used together to clean data, analyze business performance, and create an interactive dashboard for decision-making.
